Name:
Validate Floating Point Numbers

Question:
Check if a floating point number (e.g. 3.14159) is in a valid format.

Solution:
/^[-+]?(\d+[.,]?\d*|\d*[.,]\d+)([eE][-+]?\d+)?$/
